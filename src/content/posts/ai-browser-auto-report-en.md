I built an AI Browser that records every step of its own reasoning — every search query, every intermediate judgment call, automatic screenshot decisions — and compiles it all into a structured investigation report at the end.

The goal was to make the research process *auditable*. Not just "here's an answer," but "here's exactly how I got there and what I looked at along the way."
